   - Select any predefined tasbih (e.g., "Subhanallah")
   - Tap the screen to count
   - Press "Stop & Save" when done

3. **Add Custom Tasbih**
   - Open tasbih selection
   - Tap "+ Add New Tasbih"
   - Enter your tasbih name
   - Confirm to add
